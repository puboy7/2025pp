## T-Echo
All-in-one unit with E-Ink screen, GPS and battery in injection-molded case. Features the low-power nRF52840 for long battery life.

| Name            | MCU      | Radio         | WiFi         | BT  | GPS  |
|-----------------|----------|---------------|--------------|-----|------|
| T-Echo          | nRF52840 | SX1262        | NO           | 5.0 | YES  |
